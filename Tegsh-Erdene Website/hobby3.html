My ancestry

I come from a rich cultural background that has greatly influenced my appreciation for diverse traditions and practices. Growing up, I was exposed to various cultural events and celebrations that sparked my interest in learning more about different cultures around the world.

The main events of my culture

My culture is rich with traditions and celebrations that bring the community together. Some of the most significant events include Naadam, Lunar new year(Tsagaan Sar) which means white moon in Mongolian, and our Great Khan's birthday.

  • Naadam is a long held tradition about three main sports: wrestling, horse racing, and archery.
  • Lunar New Year (Tsagaan Sar) is a time for family gatherings and traditional celebrations.
  • Great Khan's birthday is a significant event that honors the legacy of our historical leaders.

Why I love it

I love my culture because it provides a strong sense of identity and belonging. The traditions and celebrations bring people together and create lasting memories.
